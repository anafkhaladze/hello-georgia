About Tskaltubo

Best time to visit : ALL SEASON

Tskaltubo, in Imereti, has been inhabited since the Stone Age. It boasts historic sites, stunning lakes, ancient caves, and the famous Sataplia Reserve with dinosaur footprints. The area is known for its healing thermal waters and a tourism-driven economy, alongside horticulture and grain production.

Geography and climate

Tskaltubo municipality, in Imereti, lies between the Rioni and Gubistskali rivers. Its center is Tskaltubo, with multiple villages and settlements. It borders Kutaisi, Samtredia, Khoni, Tsageri, Ambrolauri, Baghdati, and Vani. The area has a humid subtropical climate, with hot summers (28-30°C) and mild winters (5°C). Key water bodies include the Rioni River, Tskaltubostskali, Gubistskali, Tavshava Lake, and Cold Lake.
