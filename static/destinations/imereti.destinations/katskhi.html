About Katskhi Pillar

Best time to visit : SPRING, SUMMERR, FALL

Katskhi Pillar, located 13.4 km from Chiatura, is a striking natural wonder and a symbol of the region. Standing about 40 meters high, it has long been a place of worship, first for pagans and later for Christian monks seeking solitude. Today, only one monk lives atop the pillar, experiencing a deep sense of divine presence. Women are not allowed to visit. The site also offers climbing opportunities for those with the right equipment. A 131-ft ladder allows access, taking around 20 minutes to descend.
