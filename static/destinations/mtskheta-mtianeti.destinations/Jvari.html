About Jvari

Best season to visit : ALL SEASON

Jvari Monastery is a 6th-century church and monastery located east of Mtskheta, on a rocky mountain peak at the confluence of the Mtkvari and Aragvi rivers. It is a UNESCO World Heritage site and a significant landmark of Georgian Christianity.
