About Gudauri Ski Resort

Best time to visit: Winter, March

Gudauri Ski Resort is located in the Stepantsminda region, at an elevation of 2200 meters above the sea level. Gudauri Ski resort is 120 Km away from Tbilisi. The ski season is open from December to March.
