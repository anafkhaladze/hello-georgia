About Juta

Best time to visit: Spring, Summer, Autumn

Juta - village in Mtskheta-Mtianeti region, in Kazbegi municipality, in Sno community. Located on the southern slope of the Khevi Kavkasioni main ridge, in the valley of the river Snostskhali (Tergi right tributary). 2200 meters above sea level, 20 km from Stepantsminda.
