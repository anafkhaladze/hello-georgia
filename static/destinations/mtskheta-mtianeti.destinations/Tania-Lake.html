About Lake Tanie

Best season to visit : Summer

Tanie Lake is located in the Khevsureti region, specifically in Arkhoti, within the gorge of the Tanie River, in Dusheti Municipality. The lake is nestled between two towering mountains and is renowned for its heart-shaped form, making it especially popular among young adventurers. The lake boasts exceptionally clean water, filtered naturally from the ground, and rarely experiences overflow. In summer, the water temperature is relatively warm compared to other mountain lakes.
