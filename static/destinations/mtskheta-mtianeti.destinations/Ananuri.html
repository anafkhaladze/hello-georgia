About Ananuri

Best season to visit :: All Season

Ananuri Fortress Complex, a 16th-18th century stronghold, is located in Dusheti Municipality, Mtskheta-Mtianeti region, near the Zhinvali Reservoir. It was a key military and trade hub in Aragvi Saeristavo. The complex includes upper and lower fortresses, churches, towers, and reservoirs. The Church of the Mother of God, built in 1689, is its main landmark. Ananuri played a strategic role in Georgiaâ€™s feudal era, later becoming a royal residence. In 1812, Russian troops burned it during the Kakheti uprising.
