About Pitareti Monastery

Best season to visit : ALL SEASON

Pitareti Monastery, in Kvemo Kartli’s Tetritskaro Municipality, dates back to 1213-1222 during Lasha-George’s reign. The complex includes a church, bell tower, wine cellar, fortress wall, and tombs. It became the burial site of the Orbeliani family, including Kaplan Orbeliani, who restored it after enemy invasions. The monastery is notable for its intricate ornamentation.
