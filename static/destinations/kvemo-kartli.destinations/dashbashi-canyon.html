About Dashbashi Canyon

Best season to visit : ALL SEASON

Dashbashi Canyon, in Kvemo Kartli’s Tsalka Municipality, is a stunning natural monument in the Khrami River gorge at 1110-1448 meters above sea level. Known for its biodiversity and picturesque waterfalls, including the famous Dashbashi Waterfall, the 8 km-long canyon offers breathtaking views, caves, and lush forests—perfect for adventurers and nature lovers.
