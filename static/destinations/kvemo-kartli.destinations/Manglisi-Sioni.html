About Manglisi Sioni

Best season to visit : ALL SEASON

Manglisi Sioni, in Kvemo Kartli, dates back to the 4th century when Bishop John, sent by Constantine, began its construction. It was one of the bishoprics founded by Vakhtang Gorgasali in the 5th century. The original tetraconch design influenced Georgian architecture. Damaged in the 7th century and later rebuilt under King George I (1014-1027), the church saw final modifications in the 19th century during Russian rule.
