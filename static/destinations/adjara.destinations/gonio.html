About Gonio

Best time to visit : ALL SEANON

Gonio - is a settlement near Batumi, in Gonio - Kvariati district. It is located on the left side of the river Tchorokhi. Gonio is one of the popular Black Sea resorts in Georgia.

History

Gonio's settlement dates back to the 8th-7th century BC. The fortress Apsarunti was built in the 1st century AD, later becoming Gonio Fortress. It was under Ottoman rule from 1547 to 1878, then became Georgian after the Russian-Turkish war. Gonio became part of Batumi district and later Khelvachauri region, finally joining Batumi in 2011.
