About Makhuntseti Waterfall

Best time to visit : ALL SEASON

Makhuntseti Waterfall, one of Georgia's most popular waterfalls, is located in Adjara’s Makhuntseti village, easily accessible by car or taxi. It can be visited year-round, with locals selling honey, Churchkhela, and other products nearby. The trip from Batumi takes about an hour.
