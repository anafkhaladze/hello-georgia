About kaviani Fortress

Best time to visit : ALL SEANON

Kaviani Fortress, located in the village of Khichauri, Keda Municipality, Adjara, sits on the Kaviani hill chain. It consists of two sections: the first was a three-story storehouse with niches and two windows, while the second features a well-preserved irregular rectangular tower with three floors and windows. The western wall of the prison yard is also intact, measuring 7.1 meters in length and 5 meters in height. The fortress is constructed from flat stones and cobblestones.
