About Green Lake

Best time to visit : SUMMER, SEPTEMBER

Mtsvane Tba is located in Achara in Khulo municipality on the north side of the Arsiani mountain range, at the 2058 MSL. The lake is slightly mineralized, clean, transparent and drinkable. there are no fish in the lake.

QUESTIONS

Is it allowed to camp with tents?+
Yes it is allowed.
I'm planning to take the minibus from Tbilisi, and can you tell me what route it takes and where I should get off to walk to the Green Lake?+
To get to Green Lake, you need to go up the Goderdzi Pass, from where it is a 7 km dirt road to the lake. There is no direct transport from Tbilisi to the Goderdzi Pass, there are two roads from Tbilisi to the Goderdzi Pass, one from Akhaltsikhe and the other from Batumi.It is possible to get to the Goderdzi Pass from Batumi by transport, from Akhaltsikhe it is possible to get to Adigeni by transport, then you will need to rent a taxi or, if you are lucky, you can use the so-called hitchhiking.
