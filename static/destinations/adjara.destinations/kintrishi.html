About Kintrishi Nature Reserve

Best time to visit : ALL SEASON

Kintrishi National Park near Kobuleti offers scenic trails, historic sites, waterfalls, and rare flora. Visitors can hike or ride to Tamari Arch Bridge, Khinotsminda Church ruins, and Lake Tbikeli, with camping and picnic spots available.
