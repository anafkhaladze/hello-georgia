About Nikortsminda

Best time to visit: All Season

Nikortsminda is a remarkable domed temple located in the Racha-Lechkhumi and Kvemo Svaneti region, in the village of Nikortsminda within Ambrolauri Municipality. The church is an important cultural and architectural monument, dedicated to St. Nicholas.

History

Nikortsminda Church, built between 1010-1014, is a Georgian architectural masterpiece with a well-preserved dome and intricate façade. Renovated in the 16th century by King Bagrat III, it has remained largely unchanged. Russian ambassadors in the 18th century praised its size and beauty, noting the tombs of the king’s relatives, which were later covered during renovations.
