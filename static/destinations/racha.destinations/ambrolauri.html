About Ambrolauri

Best time to visit : All Season

Ambrolauri - City and municipality, administrative center of Racha-Lechkhumi and Kvemo Svaneti. It is located at river mouth of Rioni and Krikhula, at 550 m above sea level. There are: a wine factory, court, gym, hospital, theater, museum and a residence of Nikortsminda Eparchy.

QUESTIONS

Where is it better to climb? Glola or Shovi?+
You have to climb hills from both sides. However, from the Shovi side you have to cross a pass which is very steep. Therefore, if you don't want to cross the pass, it is better to climb from Glola.
