About Udziro Lake

Best time to visit : SUMMER, SEPTEMBER

Udziro Lake, one of several lakes with the same name, is situated in Racha, near the villages of Glola and Shovi, on the western slope of Mount Katitsvera. It rests at an altitude of 2,800 meters above sea level and offers breathtaking views of the Caucasus Mountains.

QUESTIONS

Where is it better to climb? Glola or Shovi?+
You have to climb hills from both sides. However, from the Shovi side you have to cross a pass which is very steep. Therefore, if you don't want to cross the pass, it is better to climb from Glola.
