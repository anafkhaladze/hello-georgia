ABOUT Gremi Monastery

Best time to visit : ALL SEASON

Gremi Cathedral, built in 1565 by King Levan of Kakheti, is located in Kvareli, Kakheti. Once the capital of the Kakheti Kingdom (15th–17th centuries), Gremi was a political, economic, and cultural hub. The architectural complex includes the Archangels’ Church, a three-story palace-bell tower, baths, a market, and a secret tunnel. The museum, established in 1975, holds artifacts like Bronze Age weapons, medieval ceramics, and royal portraits. Some findings are preserved in the National Museum of Georgia.
