ABOUT Sighnaghi

Sighnaghi Municipality in Kakheti, Georgia, has ancient roots, with settlements dating back to the Paleolithic. Known as "the city-museum," Sighnaghi features stunning views of the Alazani Valley and Caucasus Mountains. Key landmarks include the Bodbe and Khirsi monasteries.

Geography and climate

The municipality spans the Iori Plateau and Gombori Ridge, with the Alazani and Iori rivers. The climate varies, with hot summers and cold winters on the plateau and moderate temperatures in the valley and ridge. Average annual temperature: 11.1°C to 12.6°C.
