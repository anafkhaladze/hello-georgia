ABOUT David Gareja Monastery

Best time to visit : ALL SEASON

Davit Gareja (David Gareji Monastery Complex) - one of the most notable religious and cultural centers of Georgia, the monastery cave complex. It is located in Kakheti, Sagarejo municipality, 60-70 km south-east of Tbilisi, on Gareji hill.

History

David Gareji Monastery, founded in the 6th century by Assyrian father David, expanded into multiple monasteries over time. King David the Builder made it royal property in the 12th century, fostering its growth. Despite destruction by Mongols and Persians, it remained a key religious and cultural center, known for its frescoes of Georgian kings and religious figures. Monks and scholars preserved manuscripts and enriched Georgian literature.
