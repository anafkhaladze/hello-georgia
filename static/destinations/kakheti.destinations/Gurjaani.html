ABOUT Gurjaani

Best time to visit : ALL SEASON

Gurjaani is a town in eastern Georgia, the administrative center of Gurjaani Municipality in Kakheti. Located in the Alazani Valley at 415m above sea level, it is a key railway junction. The town has wine, canning, and food industries, a theater, a historical museum, and the N. Vachnadze House-Museum.
