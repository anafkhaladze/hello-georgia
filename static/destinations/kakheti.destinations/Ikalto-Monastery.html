ABOUT Gurjaani

Best time to visit : ALL SEASON

Ikalto Monastery, founded in the 6th century by Assyrian father Zenon, is located in Kakheti, Telavi Municipality. The complex includes three churches and the ruins of an academy, believed to be established by Arsen Ikaltoeli (11th–12th centuries). The main church, Peristsvaleba, dates to the 8th–9th centuries. The academy building, resembling feudal palaces, was likely used for scholarly gatherings.

History

Ikalto Academy ceased functioning in 1616 after Shah Abbas set the monastery on fire. It was a tomb for the Makashvili family, with notable contributions from Ioane and Gabriel Makashvili. Following Russia’s annexation in 1921, the monastery was closed. A museum opened in 1965, but many ecclesiastical items were lost. Religious services resumed in 1991, and the academy was rehabilitated between 2004–2009.
