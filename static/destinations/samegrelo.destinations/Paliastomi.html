About Paliastomi Lake

Best time to visit : Spring, Summer, Autumn

Paliastomi Lake, located near Poti in Samegrelo and partly in Guria, is part of Kolkheti National Park. It covers 18.2 km² and is the third-largest lake in Georgia. The lake is a popular bird-watching spot, serving as a key stop for migratory birds. It’s also home to a variety of fish species, including Sudak, Pike, and Common carp. Boating tours are available to explore the lake.
