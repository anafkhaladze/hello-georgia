About Intsira Waterfall (Intsra)

Best time to visit : Spring, Summer, Autumn

Intsra Waterfall, in Samegrelo’s Tsalenjikha Municipality, flows into the Intsra River Canyon, surrounded by mossy rocks and box trees. It’s 1.6 km from Chkvaleri village by car, followed by a short but steep forest trail. Nearby, Intsra Cave has a spring that guides visitors along the path.
