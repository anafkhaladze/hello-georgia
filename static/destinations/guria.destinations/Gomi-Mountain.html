About Gomi Mountain

Best time to visit : SUMMER, SEPTEMBER

Gomismta is a mountainous summer resort in Guria, situated 2100-2755 meters above sea level. Surrounded by spruce and fir forests, it offers natural healing effects with mineral waters and a spa-like climate. The tourist season is short, lasting only during the summer months. There’s no infrastructure, electricity, or piped water; locals rely on nearby mountain springs. Wooden cottages are available for rent, but there are no hotels, markets, or other amenities.

In August, the "Gomismtoba" festival takes place, featuring sports competitions and horse races. The resort is known for its breathtaking views, especially the "sunset in the clouds" or "sunrise from the mist." Nearby, Chinchao Lake, at 2500 meters, is accessible via a dirt road. The climate is cold in winter and moderately warm in summer.

The resort is 350 km from Tbilisi and 33 km from Ozurgeti. While there’s no direct public transport to Gomismta, buses and trains go to Ozurgeti, from where taxis or hitchhiking can get you to the resort.
