About Lake Jaji

Best time to visit : SUMMER, MAY, SEPTEMBER

Jaji Lake is located on the southern slope of the Meskheti Range at 2655 meters above sea level. The lake is at the crossroad of three municipalities: Chokhatauri, Vani and Adigeni. Near the lake is the peak Mepistskaro, which is 2850 meters high and offers travelers views of three different regions: Imereti, Guria and Samtskhe-Javakheti.

There are several routes to the lake: 1. Abastumani; 2. Phersati plateau; 3. The village of Derceli.

From the given options, it is possible to get closer to the lake, from the summer farms of Derceli village because this is the closest point where you can get by car.

The longest route starts from Abastumani Observatory. Starting the route from Abastumani is convenient for small groups because it is easy to get to the Abastumani Observatory by public transport. The trail follows a ridge and offers travelers spectacular views on both sides.
