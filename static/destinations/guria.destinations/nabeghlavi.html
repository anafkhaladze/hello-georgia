About Nabeghlavi

Best time to visit : SUMMER

Nabeglavi is a village in Chokhatauri Municipality, Guria Region, Georgia, the center of the community . It is located on the right bank of the Gubazeuli River, 475 m above sea level, 22 km from Chokhatauri, 40 km from Sajavakho railway station. The Chokhatauri-Bakhmaro road passes through the village. There is a public school in the village.
