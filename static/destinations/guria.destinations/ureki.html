About Ureki

Best time to visit : SUMMER

Ureki is a town and seaside resort in Georgia, in the Ozurgeti municipality of the Guria region. It is located on the Black Sea coast, 4 m above sea level, 24 km from Ozurgeti. It is bordered from the north and south by the confluence of the Sefa and Supsa rivers, and from the east by Mount Tsvermagala. There is a railway station in Ureki, the S2 highway passes through, there is a kindergarten, a public school, hotels and other institutions.
