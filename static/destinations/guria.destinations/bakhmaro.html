About Bakhmaro

Best time to visit : SUMMER

Bakhmaro is a mountain resort in Guria, known for its fresh air and healing conditions. Surrounded by spruce and fir forests, it’s a popular spa destination, mainly active in summer. The resort lacks infrastructure like water supply, and reaching it in winter is difficult, but ferryboats and snow-cars are available.

The average temperature is 14°C in summer and -5°C in winter. Bakhmaro is 52 km from Chokhatauri and 340 km from Tbilisi, accessible by asphalt roads. The village offers wooden homes for rent, a hotel, small shops, cafes, and parks. Visitors gather around campfires to enjoy the night sky.

Bakhmaro is also known for its stunning sunsets from Gadrekili Mountain, horsemanship, and local products like Kaimaghi (a creamy treat) and spruce chewing-gum. It’s a perfect spot to experience nature’s beauty.
