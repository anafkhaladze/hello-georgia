About Tetnuldi

Best time to visit : All Season

Tetnuldi ski resort is located on the slopes of Svaneti Caucasus mountain range, in the municipality of Mestia, Zemo Svaneti. Tetnuldi is the second largest ski resort in Georgia, after Gudauri. The maximum elevation of ski tracks is 3165, and the minimum elevation is 2260 meters MSL. Tetnuldi is 15 Km away from Mestia and the average time of transportation is 1 hour
