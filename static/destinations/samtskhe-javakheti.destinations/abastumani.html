About Abastumani

Best time to visit : All Season

Abastumani- the resort in southern Georgia, in Samtskhe-Javakheti region, Adigeni municipality. It is located on the south slopes of Meskheti Ridge, in the valley of river Otskhe. The resort is 25km away from Adigeni and 28km away from Akhaltsikhe.

In Abastumani, there are tuberculosis sanatoriums, astrophysical observatories, cultural, educational and spa centers.
