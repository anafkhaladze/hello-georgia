About Vardzia

Best time to visit: All Season

Vardzia is a 12th-13th century monastery complex carved into rock in Aspindza, Javakheti, 30 km from Aspindza, at 1,300 meters above sea level. It spans 100 meters in height, with 3 to 13 storeys, including over 600 rooms, storage facilities, 25 wine cellars, and 185 wine jars. The highest cave is 1,462 meters above sea level.
