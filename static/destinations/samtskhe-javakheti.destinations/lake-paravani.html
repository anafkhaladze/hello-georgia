About Lake Paravani

Best time to visit: All Season

Paravani is a volcanic lake in Samtskhe-Javakheti, 2,073 meters above sea level. It freezes in winter, with ice up to 73 cm thick. The lake is rich in fish and fed by several rivers. Nearby villages include Poka, Vladimirovka, and Rodionovka, with notable sites like Poka Monastery, Shaori Fortress, and Paravani caravansary.
