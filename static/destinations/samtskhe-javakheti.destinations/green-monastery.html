About About Green Monastery

Best time to visit: All Season

The Green Monastery (Chitakhevi St. George Monastery), located near the village of Chitakhevi, a few kilometers from Borjomi, dates back to the 9th century. Built by Christopher and Tevdore, students of St. Grigol Khandtstheli, the complex includes three basilica-type churches and a bell tower. The monastery was raided in the 16th century by Shah Tahmasp’s forces, with monks tortured and killed. Miraculous relics and traces of blood from this event are still visible. The church was rehabilitated in the 1980s, and the Father’s Monastery was restored in 2002.
