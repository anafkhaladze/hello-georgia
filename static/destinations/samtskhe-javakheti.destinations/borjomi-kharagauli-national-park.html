About Borjomi-Kharagauli National Park

Best time to visit: All Season

Borjomi-Kharagauli National Park in central Georgia covers 107,083 hectares and protects important ecosystems. It offers 12 hiking trails, with shelters and picnic areas, and activities like hiking, horseback riding, cycling, and cultural tours. Visitor centers are in Borjomi and Kharagauli.
